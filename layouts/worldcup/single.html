How The Draw Works

Each player receives one team from the Big Guns, one from the Wild Cards, and one from the Chaos Picks. The draw shuffles each pot separately, then deals the first shuffled team in each pot to the first player, the second to the second player, and so on.

Tiers are based on the FIFA/Coca-Cola Men's World Ranking table from FIFA, using the 48 teams present in the openfootball fixture data. Those teams are sorted by FIFA rank, then split into three equal groups of 16: Big Guns, Wild Cards, and Chaos Picks.
