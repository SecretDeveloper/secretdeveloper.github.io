The Milky War

Game

Alien strike groups are massing inside the asteroid belt for a direct assault on your home planet.

Your mission is to break each staging force before it can launch, push through the belt sector by sector, and stop the invasion before it reaches orbit.

Press Enter to launch immediately, or review the field guide and controls first.

Information

Strike Fighter Your interceptor. Push into each staging zone, break the alien formation, and escape before reinforcements arrive.
Machine Gun High-rate tracer fire for shredding fighters and finishing weakened targets.
Nukes Heavy strike rockets that deliver oversized blast damage to command ships and clustered threats.
Targeting Missile Guided ordnance that locks onto priority targets weaving through debris fields.
Shield Recharge Restores your defensive envelope and can overcharge it when picked up at full strength.
Enemy Fighter Fast attack craft that circle and fire into the asteroid field. Clearing them opens the warp route.
Enemy Command Ship Sector commander with escorts. Destroying it collapses the alien hold on miniboss sectors.

Controls

• Left/Right Arrow or A/D steers your fighter.
• Up Arrow or W engages thrust.
• Space fires the active weapon.
• Enter launches, resumes, or restarts the run.
• Enemy ships are the objective; asteroids are lethal environmental hazards.

Paused

Press Enter to resume the defense run.
